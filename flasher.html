Help: Connecting & Configuring

          
        

        

          
Connect your ESP32 board and select GhostESP firmware.

          
    
            
  • Connect via a USB data cable (not charge-only)
    • 
            
  • Put the board in bootloader mode: hold BOOT, press RESET, release BOOT
    • 
            
  • Click Connect Device and select the serial port
    • 
            
  • Select your preferred GhostESP build from the dropdown
    • 
            
  • Firmware will download and extract automatically
    •
