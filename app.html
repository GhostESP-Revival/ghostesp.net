GhostESP Companion

Take full control of your GhostESP device directly from your Android phone. Monitor WiFi networks, track BLE devices, manage NFC tags, and more - all from a sleek mobile interface.

Get it on Google Play View Source

Features

WiFi Scanner

Scan for nearby WiFi networks, view channel congestion, track access points, and capture handshakes.

BLE Monitor

Monitor Bluetooth Low Energy devices, detect Flipper Zero units, track GATT services, and wardrive.

NFC Tools

Read and write NFC tags, manage saved tags, and integrate with Chameleon Ultra support.

IR Control

Learn and transmit infrared signals, browse remote databases, and control your devices seamlessly.

GPS Wardriving

Log WiFi and BLE networks with GPS coordinates for wardriving adventures and network mapping.

BadUSB

Execute BadUSB attacks directly from your phone with easy-to-use scripts and payloads.

SD Manager

Browse and manage files on your GhostESP SD card, upload and download with ease.

Terminal

Full serial console access to execute commands and interact with your GhostESP device.

Evil Portal

Create and manage captive portal attacks with easy configuration and credential harvesting.

Settings

Configure device preferences, theme options, privacy modes, and connection settings.

Screens

A clean, intuitive interface designed for efficiency

Requirements

Android 8.0+

Requires Android Oreo or higher for full functionality

USB OTG Cable

On-the-go cable to connect your phone to GhostESP

GhostESP Device

Any GhostESP-compatible ESP32 board running firmware

Enable USB Debugging

Developer options must be enabled for serial communication
