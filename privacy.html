Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

GhostESP ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit ghostesp.net, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

Analytics (Google Analytics)

With your consent, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search engine
  • General geographic location (country/region level)
  • Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • Anonymized IP address

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally. We use it solely to improve the website experience.

Information You Provide

If you submit feedback through our feedback form, we collect the information you choose to provide (topic, message, and optional contact details).

Functional Cookies

We use a small number of strictly functional cookies that do not require consent:

  • Cookie consent preference — remembers your analytics consent choice
  • Dark mode preference — stores your theme setting (in the embedded flash tool)

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Understand which pages and features are most useful
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Improve the overall user experience

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Analytics data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

Your Rights & Choices

  • Consent: You can accept or reject analytics cookies via the consent banner shown on your first visit
  • Change preference: Click "Cookie Settings" in the footer of any page to update your choice at any time
  • Do Not Track / Global Privacy Control: We honor both the DNT browser header and the GPC signal. If either is enabled, analytics cookies are automatically rejected and any previously set analytics cookies are cleared — no banner is shown
  • Data deletion: Analytics data is automatically deleted after 26 months. You can request immediate deletion by contacting us

Third-Party Links

Our site links to external websites (GitHub, Discord, Instagram, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your data rights, you can reach us at partners@ghostesp.net or on Discord.
